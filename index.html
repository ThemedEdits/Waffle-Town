Our Story

Delicious Waffles Since 2015

Waffle Town was founded by two childhood friends with a passion for creating the perfect waffle. What started as a small food truck has grown into a beloved local destination for waffle lovers of all ages.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients - organic flour, free-range eggs, and real butter - to create waffles that are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. Every waffle is made to order with love and attention to detail.

Our mission is simple: to bring joy to our community one waffle at a time. Whether you're stopping by for breakfast, lunch, or just a sweet treat, we promise an experience that will make you smile.
